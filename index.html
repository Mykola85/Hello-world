Scotland

Scotlandis a country that is part of the United Kingdom.Covering the northern third of the island of Great Britain mainland Scotland has a 96-mile border with England to the southeast and is otherwise surrounded by the Atlantic Ocean to the north and west,the North Sea to the northeast and the Irish Sea to the south. The country also contains more than 790 islands, principally in the archipelagos of the Hebrides and the Northern Isles.

Recognised languages

  • English Scots
  • Scottish Gaelic
  • British Sign Language
